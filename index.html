How to Get to Our Clinic from Markham

Our clinic is at 1520 Steeles Ave W, Unit 101A, Vaughan, ON L4K 3B9, on the northwest corner of Dufferin Street and Steeles Avenue West.

  • By car (fastest): About 20 minutes (~20 km) from downtown Markham via Highway 407. The quickest route, though Highway 407 is a toll road.
  • By car (toll-free): About 28 minutes (18 km) via Highway 7, or roughly 30 minutes straight west along Steeles Avenue — no tolls.
  • By transit: About 52 minutes on York Region Transit / Viva (e.g. routes 304 and 60A), starting near Enterprise Boulevard in downtown Markham.
  • By bike: About 1 hour (18.5 km), mostly along Highway 7.

Frequently Asked Questions. Therapy for Markham

Do you have a clinic in Markham?

Our physical clinic is in Vaughan, at Dufferin and Steeles, about a 20-minute drive from downtown Markham. We serve Markham clients in two ways: in person at the Vaughan office, and through secure online therapy anywhere in Ontario.

How much does therapy cost, and is it covered by insurance?

Sessions start at $80, and your first assessment is free. Coverage varies by insurer. Private psychotherapy is generally not covered by OHIP.

Can I see a Markham therapist online instead of travelling to Vaughan?

Yes. Virtual counselling is available across Markham and all of Ontario, with the same therapists as in-person sessions, just over private video.

Do I need a referral to start?

No referral is needed. You can book directly and begin with a free initial assessment.

What kinds of therapy do you offer?

Our team uses evidence-based approaches including CBT, EFT, DBT, ACT and EMDR, tailored to your goals. We do not promise guaranteed outcomes — we focus on practical, lasting skills.

Are sessions confidential?

Yes. Whether you meet us in person or online, your sessions are private and confidential, in keeping with CRPO standards.

Do you offer couples or marriage counselling for Markham clients?

Yes. Our relationship therapists support Markham couples with communication, conflict and connection, available in person or online.

Are your therapists registered?

Yes. Our team includes psychotherapists registered with the College of Registered Psychotherapists of Ontario (CRPO), Ontario's regulatory college for the profession.
